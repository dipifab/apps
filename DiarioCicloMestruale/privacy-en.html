1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. Diario Ciclo Mestruale ("the App") was designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data in compliance with EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR).

In short: Your menstrual cycle data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

3. Data Collected and Purposes

Diario Ciclo Mestruale allows you to record information about your menstrual cycle, including:

  • Menstrual cycle start and end dates
  • Flow intensity
  • Symptoms (pain, mood, energy, etc.)
  • Personal notes
  • Customized reminders and notifications

Processing Purposes

The data you enter in the app is used exclusively to:

Legal Basis

The processing of your data is based on your consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR), which you provide by using the app and voluntarily entering your data. Since menstrual cycle data is considered health data (special category of data, Art. 9 GDPR), processing occurs exclusively on your device, without any transmission to third parties.

5. iCloud Sync

If you choose to use iCloud sync:

  • Your data is transferred to Apple servers via encrypted connection
  • Apple manages this data according to their own Privacy Policy
  • Only you can access your data through your Apple ID
  • You can disable sync at any time from iOS Settings

To manage or delete data synced to iCloud:

  1. Go to Settings on your device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Select iCloud
  4. Find Diario Ciclo Mestruale in the app list
  5. Disable sync or manage storage
