1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. Luna ("the App") was designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

In short: Your menstrual cycle data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

2. Data We Collect

Luna allows you to record information about your menstrual cycle, including:

  • Menstrual cycle start and end dates
  • Flow intensity
  • Symptoms (pain, mood, energy, etc.)
  • Personal notes
  • Customized reminders and notifications

  • Usage data or analytics

  • Payment information

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Data you enter in Luna is stored in two possible locations:

üì± On Your Device

All data is saved locally on your iPhone or iPad using CoreData, Apple's secure storage system. The data is protected by device encryption and accessible only when the device is unlocked.

‚òÅÔ∏è In Your iCloud (Optional)

If you have enabled iCloud sync on your device, data is synced to your personal iCloud account to allow you to access it from other Apple devices.

Important: We do not have our own servers. We cannot access your data in any way, whether local or on iCloud.

4. iCloud Sync

If you choose to use iCloud sync:

  • Your data is transferred to Apple servers via encrypted connection
  • Apple manages this data according to their own Privacy Policy
  • Only you can access your data through your Apple ID
  • You can disable sync at any time from iOS Settings

To manage or delete data synced to iCloud:

  1. Go to Settings on your device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Select iCloud
  4. Find Luna in the app list
  5. Disable sync or manage storage

7. Children

Luna is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children's data.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 is using the app, we recommend supervising device usage through Apple's Parental Controls features.
