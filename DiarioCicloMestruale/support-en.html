If you have iCloud enabled on your device and have enabled sync for Diario Ciclo e Calendario, your data will automatically sync between all your Apple devices connected to the same Apple ID. You can manage this setting in:

To delete local data:

  1. Uninstall the app from your device

To also delete iCloud data:

  1. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage
  2. Find Diario Ciclo e Calendario and tap Delete Data

Diario Ciclo e Calendario calculates predictions based on your previous cycle data. The more data you enter, the more accurate the predictions will be. However, every body is different and many factors can affect your cycle (stress, travel, lifestyle changes, etc.).

Consider predictions as indicative estimates, not certainties.
