Your health, your privacy

Diario Ciclo Mestruale lets you track your menstrual cycle in a simple, intuitive, and completely private way.

Privacy First

Your data never leaves your device. We have no servers, we don't collect information, we don't sell anything. Your data is yours alone.

Key Features

Cycle Tracking

Record the start and end of your cycle with just a few taps. The app automatically calculates the average duration and predicts your next cycles.

Symptom Logging

Note your daily symptoms: mood, pain, energy, and much more. Everything organized in a clear timeline.

Statistics and Insights

View your cycle trends with intuitive charts. Discover patterns and correlations over time.

iCloud Sync

Your data automatically syncs between your Apple devices through your personal iCloud account.
